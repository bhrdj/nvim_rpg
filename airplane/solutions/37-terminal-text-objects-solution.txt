TERMINAL TEXT OBJECTS - SOLUTION
================================

1. Word under cursor
   yiw

2. WORD under cursor (whitespace-delimited)
   yiW

3. Inside double quotes
   yi"

4. Inside single quotes
   yi'

5. Inside parentheses
   yi(  or  yib

6. Inside square brackets
   yi[

7. Inside curly braces
   yi{  or  yiB

8. Entire current line
   yy

Remember:
  i = inner (not including delimiters)
  a = around (including delimiters)

So ya" yanks "hello" including the quotes,
while yi" yanks just hello.
