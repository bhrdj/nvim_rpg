MULTI-WINDOW TERMINAL WORKFLOW - SOLUTION
=========================================

1. From buffer, go to terminal window
   Ctrl-w l  (if terminal is right)
   Ctrl-w w  (cycle to next)

2. From terminal, go to buffer window
   Ctrl-w h  (if buffer is left)
   Ctrl-w w  (cycle)

3. Make terminal window wider
   Ctrl-w >  (repeat or use count: 10 Ctrl-w >)

4. Make buffer window taller
   (go to buffer first)  Ctrl-w +

5. Equalize window sizes
   Ctrl-w =

6. Maximize terminal window temporarily
   Ctrl-w _  (height)
   Ctrl-w |  (width)
   (Ctrl-w = to restore)

7. Close terminal window (keep buffer)
   (in terminal window)  :q  or  :bd!

8. Reopen terminal in bottom split
   (from buffer)  :below sp | terminal
   or: :sp | terminal  then  Ctrl-w J
